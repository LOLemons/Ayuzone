Indigestion Remedy

Use Case: Indigestion



Ingredients:


- 5g Crushed Rhizome


- salt/jaggery for taste (whichever you prefer)

Steps:



1. Mix the crushed rhizome and salt/jaggery well


2. Take twice daily before meals


3. These preparations are for mild and chronic conditions. In case patient does not get relief within 2-3 days of taking medicine, they may consult the nearest doctor.
